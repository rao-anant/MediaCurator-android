Privacy Policy

App: Media Curator
Developer: Anant Rao
Last updated: May 2025

Overview

Media Curator is a local media management app. It helps you browse, curate, and delete photos, videos, and PDFs stored on your device. No data ever leaves your device. The app has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no internet connection.

What the app accesses

  • Photos and videos — read from your device's MediaStore to display thumbnails and allow deletion.
  • PDF files — read from publicly accessible storage locations (Downloads, Documents, etc.) to display and allow deletion.
  • Downloads folder (write) — used solely to save and restore a local backup file (mediacurator_hidden.json) that records which months you have marked as done. This file stays on your device.

What the app does NOT do

  • Does not collect any personal data.
  • Does not transmit any data over the internet.
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • Does not access your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.
  • Does not share any data with third parties.

Permissions explained

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO — required to display your photos and videos.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — used on Android 12 and below for the same purpose.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — used on Android 9 and below to save the backup file.
  • MANAGE_MEDIA — optional. When granted, allows deleting media without a confirmation dialog on each operation (Android 12+). You can enable or disable this in the app's menu.
  • All files access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — required on Android 11+ to browse PDF files stored in Downloads, Documents, and other shared folders. Android's granular media permissions (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO) cover only photos and videos; there is no standard permission for reading PDFs across folders on Android 13+. Photos and videos work without this permission. The app does not use this permission to collect, upload, or share any data.

Data storage

The only file the app writes is mediacurator_hidden.json in your Downloads folder. It contains a list of year-month keys you have marked as done (e.g. "2023-04"). It contains no photos, no personal information, and no device identifiers. You can delete it at any time.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
